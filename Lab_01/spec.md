# Time Dash 

## Core Mechanics

- 1 coin Spawns within the Map at a time
- Avoid 2-3 enemies within the map 
- Get as much coins as possible 

## Player Actions 

- WASD movement 
- Mouse Look around 
- Sprint 

## Enemy Behaviour 

- Follow player position 

## Win / Loss Condition

- death by enemy (Lose)
- not collecting coins in time (lose)
- collecting coins 10 (Win)
